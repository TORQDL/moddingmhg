In-depth Modding of MH: TCatS Steam Version - Creating a Nation
By Ted52
This Guide will show you how to create a completely new nation in your scenario mainly using the Making History Editor. This Guide will cover the creation of the nation, the selection of pre existing supplies and how to bind in your own emblems and flags.

Introduction
This guide will introduce you to the first part of modding an own nation or altering a pre existing one for your custom scenario of Making History: The Calm and the Storm. This guide will work for Steam Version, retail non-gold version and mostly gold version as well, but of course it is written using the Steam Version.

First off, youll want to create your own scenario and decide which nation to add/alter. In this case, Ill create the scenario of "Tutorial Guide" after the pre existing 'End of Diplomacy'. I will edit in the Nation of Scotland for this example.
The Basics
First, go to "Nations", click "Add" and enter the name of the country.












Then, you would want to set the regions of the world map to be owned by the nation. In this case, Ill edit the region of Scotland to be owned and controlled by the Nation of Scotland rather than the United Kingdom.









Now it looks like this. You want to move out United Kingdom's troops. There are naval units in the ports and infantry in Scotland, they have to be moved out.











Now look at that. How to change the base color will be on part 2 because you need to go into XML files and this will be restricted to the second part as I said.









Now edit the Capital (Edinburgh), the Government (Democratic), the preset technologies (Early Tanks, Basic Fighters, Improved Fighters, Improved Infantry etc.) and how much money (0) and transport ships (10) the nation has in the beginning.
Editing Flags and Emblems
For this, I mostly use the website of Making History (they have a large mod section there) and especially this download shown in the picture.








Go to Steamapps folder. Now create all the folders required for this folder line shown to the right.

Now put in the nation's flag in Steamapps > common > Making History > Modern Europe > system > scenarios > [your scenario] > ui > art > 2D > countries > [country name].

Note that country name has to equal the created nation's name and low case only. For example: scotland.

NOT Scotland, SCOTLAND, Republic of Scotland etc.
Finally
Now go to your scenario in the Game itself and select Scotland. Note that you can just set your nation to playable in the game and it will be shown in the upper lane next to China, Germany etc.







THERE YOU GO! :D Note that the flags are wrong and all because of the package I use, not because of me not knowing the flag of Scotland.
Closing remarks
What is coming in part 2?: Changing the base color of your nation, change the diplomatic relations to other countries, maybe some more concerning the Editor, but mostly editing XML files, more in depth on the image files in the country's folder.

In the editor, your countries will NEVER have their flags and emblems shown, but they will show up in the game, so do not worry.

If you want to alter a pre existing nation's flag instead of adding your own nation, simply put a folder with its name (for example "germany") and put the files in there.
