Hello. There's two ways to make flags and roundels to the nations.

Go to...\SteamApps\common\Making History The Calm and The Storm\Modern Europe\system\UI\art\2D\countries, and make a copy of one of those country folders. If you're making country art for modern Germany, then rename it for example ''Germany2''. Then you can edit those files in ''Germany2'' with GIMP to represent modern Germany's flags and aircraft roundels.

Then go to the folder ...\SteamApps\common\Making History The Calm and The Storm\Modern Europe\system\scenarios\what-ever-your-scenario\, and open the file ''countryGermany'' and edit the part:

    property name="strFlagName" value="Germany" (replace ''Germany'' with ''Germany2'', so the game uses the files in Germany2 folder)

The other way is to make new art folders in your own scenario. ...\SteamApps\common\Making History The Calm and The Storm\Modern Europe\system\scenarios\what-ever-your-scenario\ui\art\2D\countries.Then you can put the file ''Germany2'' there and edit countryGermany to use ''Germany2'' just like I explained above.

Ask me if you have any questions about this.
